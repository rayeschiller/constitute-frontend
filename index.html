Visualizations of Toxicity in Political Tweets

Toxicity Timeline

Each Tweet has three markers that represent the score for toxicity, sexually explicit, and identity attack. The larger the marker the higher the score. Use the dropdown to select a politician and view recent Tweets about them with each Tweets toxicity, sexually explicit, and identity attack scores. Select a date to view Tweets from that day. Zoom in to see each tweet. Tweets shown are a sample of total Tweets.


Percentage of Political Toxic Tweets

Toggle between absolute number and percentage of toxic, sexually explicit, and identity attack tweets directed at each politician, defined as tweets with respective scores above .7.

Comparing Attributes

In order to understand how the attributes sexually explicit, identity attack, and toxic interact with each other, select the attributes you wish to plot.
